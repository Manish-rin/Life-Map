The Problem

People are dying because of a coordination gap.

"The doctor says we need AB negative, urgent. We have no idea where to look. Everyone is calling everyone. It's been two hours."

Donors exist. Blood banks exist. But there's no intelligent, real-time system connecting them. Existing apps expose donor numbers publicly, causing spam — driving donors off platforms. Blood bank lists are outdated. WhatsApp groups are noisy. And in a critical emergency, chaos kills.

How It Works

From emergency to connection
in under 3 minutes.

A single tap broadcasts to every nearby donor instantly. The system escalates automatically if no one responds. Nobody is left without a next step.

01

Select blood group & urgency

Choose the required blood group, set a search radius (2km, 5km, or 10km), and mark urgency — Planned, Urgent, or Critical. Add the hospital name if available.

02

All nearby donors alerted instantly

One tap broadcasts a push notification to every available matching donor within your radius simultaneously — anonymous by default, showing only blood group, distance, and hospital.

03

Mutual contact revealed on acceptance

A donor accepts, and both parties see each other's contact simultaneously — a consensual, mutual reveal. No number is ever visible without explicit acceptance from both sides.

Privacy Architecture

Two modes. Consent-driven.
Automatic escalation.

Praan-setu uses a two-step privacy system. Requester contact is shared instantly for speed; full mutual reveal only happens if the donor has pre-consented — balancing privacy with the urgency of saving a life.

1
Mode 1 — Default

One-Way Reveal

Requester's number is pushed to the donor immediately — enabling them to call without delay. The donor's identity stays private until they choose to respond.

  • Requester's number → donor instantly
  • Donor's privacy fully protected
  • 90-second window before escalation
2
Mode 2 — Full Mutual Reveal

Full Mutual Reveal (Pre-Consented)

Both contacts become visible when the donor has pre-consented during registration: "In case of emergency, you may share my details with a nearby patient." No further action required.

  • Full mutual reveal — donor pre-consented
  • Auto-activates after Mode 1 expires
  • Consent revocable anytime in settings
